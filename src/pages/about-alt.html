Our Story

Legacy Concierge was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality in-home healthcare with compassion and dignity. What started as a small team of dedicated nurses has grown into one of Florida's most trusted home healthcare providers.

Our founder, Dr. Sarah Smith, recognized the need for personalized, professional care that allows patients to recover and thrive in the comfort of their own homes. Today, we serve hundreds of families across Florida, maintaining that same commitment to excellence and compassionate care.

Every member of our team is carefully selected not just for their clinical expertise, but for their genuine caring nature and dedication to patient well-being.
